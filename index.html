About me



Hi. My name is Rachele Stagni.
I'm an aspiring visual designer studying at the second year of BA in Visual Communication Design at Politecnico in Milan.
I'm strongly interested in editorial design, illustration and branding. Since I was a child I had the chance to spend my free time in artistic situations. This kind of formation stimulated a lot my creative modus operandi.
Thanks to my linguistic studies during high school, I learnt how to approach with people from different cultures. Travelling helped me fight my most shy side, now I love knowing new people and discovering new places, that can also inspire my work.
